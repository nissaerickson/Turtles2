Sea Turtle Love

I first fell in love with sea turtles snorkeling in Hawaii. Their grace and fluidity underwater is mesmorizing. Then, when I visited a turtle sanctuary in Bali, my love affair continued and I adopted a baby turtle and set it free into the ocean. My love for turtles only increased as I watched the little fella swim away. Ever since then, I've made it my goal to learn more about these mysterious animals. Honu are revered in Hawaiian culture and in cultures around the world.

Turtles are dependent on the health of our oceans and it is our responsibility to preserve and protect their marine habitat. The future of our environment relies on the health of our oceans. We can each make a difference with education and action.

What can you do to help?

  1. Apply reef-safe sunscreen. Protect your skin, but also protect coral reefs. To view a list of "reef-safe sunscreens" visit: Best Beach Sunscreen
  2. Reduce your use of plastic products.
  3. If you're in the ocean, don't step on coral. It's alive!
  4. Support organizations that help turtles.
